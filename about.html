Overview

Team Levitate Hyperloop is an emerging contributor in India’s hyperloop ecosystem, dedicated to developing a fast and sustainable high-speed transit system. As VIT Vellore’s official Hyperloop team, we provide valuable research and learning opportunities for students to apply their engineering skills in real-world projects within a hands-on, collaborative environment. Our mission is focused on building a solid foundation in hyperloop science and technology through research and design, gradually contributing meaningful insights to the field. We are one of the few stakeholders in India committed to addressing transportation challenges and advancing sustainable solutions for the future of mobility.

Our Institution



Vellore Institute of Technology has established a strong legacy of academic distinction and talent development. From its founding, it has consistently been recognized as one of the country’s leading educational institutions, playing a key role in shaping future leaders and innovators. With renowned faculty, cutting-edge infrastructure, and a commitment to research, VIT provides a thriving environment for students to excel in their chosen fields. The institution has earned a reputation for producing graduates who go on to make a global impact, synonymous with high-quality education and international recognition. VIT’s unwavering focus on fostering an innovative culture and commitment to excellence equips students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in an ever-evolving, competitive world, solidifying its esteemed position in the academic landscape.
