Team Projects

800 Mine Project

The 800 Mine Project studies latency in networked environments to enhance safety, efficiency, and sustainability. Focusing on real-time monitoring, risk assessments, and feedback, it aims to improve performance, backup and recovery, and set benchmarks for smart infrastructure.

Hyperloop Risk Analytics and Scheduler

Combines deep learning and statistical models to identify network pathways and risks, optimize schedules, and prevent disruptions in large-scale hyperloop networks. While it was conceived with the hyperloop ecosystem in mind, its applications may be widely applicable across the logistics and transportation industry.

Magnetic and Superconducting Materials Science Project

With application in energy, transportation, and computing industries, this project focuses on efficient and scalable superconductors, magnets, and energy storage materials.
